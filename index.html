Biography

Nhyoo Bajracharya (born 15 September 1964) is a celebrated Nepali music composer, lyricist, and playback singer known for his versatile contributions to Nepali, Newari, Hindi, and Chinese music industries.

Alternate Professional Name: Nhyoo Bajracharya (II) – Actor

Languages: Nepali, Newari, Hindi, Chinese

Genres: Film Score, Playback Singing, Background Music

With over three decades in the music industry, Nhyoo Bajracharya has composed soulful melodies that have resonated with audiences across cultural boundaries. His work blends traditional Nepali musical elements with contemporary arrangements, creating a unique sound that has defined many popular films and albums.

His legacy includes memorable compositions for numerous Nepali films, contributing significantly to the growth and recognition of Nepali music both nationally and internationally.
